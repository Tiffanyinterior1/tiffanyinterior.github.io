Amane Wellness

Project Type: Wellness Interior

Location: Concept Proposal

Year: 2025

Amane Wellness explores the relationship between materiality, light, and sensory calm. The project focuses on creating an intimate retreat that balances softness and structure, encouraging slow movement and mindful presence through layered textures, muted tones, and controlled lighting.
