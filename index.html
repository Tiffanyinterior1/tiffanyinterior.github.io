About

I am an interior designer drawn to exploring the dynamics of design and how spaces can be tailored to reflect unique needs and personalities of each client. I am passionate about creating intimate environements that are both functional and emotionally resonant, using materials, colours and sensory elements to evoke a deep connection wiht the space and the client. Sustainability is a key focus in my work. I believe it is essential to design with an awareness of the environement, finding ways to integrate aspects into every project. Additionally, as someone who is half Cantonese and half English, i am inspired by cultural diversity and how traditions and aesthetics can inform yet also elevate a design.
